🌬️ Breathing Exercises

🔵 4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7, exhale for 8. Repeat 4 times.

  1. Inhale (4 seconds)
  2. Hold (7 seconds)
  3. Exhale (8 seconds)
🔵 Box Breathing

Inhale-hold-exhale-hold for 4 seconds each. Repeat 4 times.

  1. Inhale (4s)
  2. Hold (4s)
  3. Exhale (4s)
  4. Hold (4s)
🔵 Alternate Nostril Breathing

Alternate breathing through one nostril at a time to calm your mind.

  1. Close right nostril, inhale left
  2. Close left nostril, exhale right
  3. Inhale right
  4. Close right, exhale left
