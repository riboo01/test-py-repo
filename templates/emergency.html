💡 How to Support Someone in Crisis
  • Stay with them and listen without judgment
  • Remove potential means of self-harm if safe
  • Contact any of the crisis resources above
  • Encourage them to speak with a mental health professional
  • Offer to help them attend appointments
🌿 Self-Care During a Crisis

If you’re feeling overwhelmed, remember:

  • Take deep breaths and slow your thoughts
  • Talk to someone you trust
  • Take a break from overwhelming news or screens
  • Rest, hydrate, and don’t isolate yourself
